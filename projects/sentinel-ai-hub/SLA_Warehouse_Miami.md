# Service Level Agreement: Miami Regional Warehouse

## 1. Disruption Coverage
This warehouse is covered for disruptions including but not limited to hurricanes, floods, and power outages.

## 2. Hurricane Response Plan
In the event of a Category 3 or higher hurricane:
- All shipments must be diverted to the Georgia hub.
- Inventory must be secured in the high-bay storage.
- The warehouse manager must report status within 2 hours of the event alert.

## 3. Liability
The company is not liable for delays exceeding 48 hours if the hurricane is declared a State of Emergency.
