## ARTICLE IV: FORCE MAJEURE AND BORDER DISRUPTIONS
In the event of a Force Majeure event including but not limited to labor strikes, pandemics, or extreme weather conditions over Category 3, the Contractor shall be granted a 72-hour grace period for all deliveries scheduled within the affected radius.

## ARTICLE XII: LIQUIDATED DAMAGES
Failure to meet the Primary Backup Hub diversion timeline will result in a penalty of $5,000 USD per day of delay. If the event is a declared "State of Emergency," these damages are waived for the first 5 days.
